Transformers module
===================

The pipelines module contains the definitions of our scikit-learn compliant preprocessing steps i.e. transformers.
Transformers are estimators supporting transform and/or fit_transform methods see `Dataset transformations <https://scikit-learn.org/stable/data_transforms.html>`_ ,
`scikit-lego <https://scikit-lego.netlify.app/>`_ and `feature-engine <https://feature-engine.readthedocs.io/en/1.0.x/>`_ for collections of transformers.
